Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 14, 2026

Workout Circuit is a fitness app that helps you build and run workout circuits. This policy explains what data the app handles.

Data we collect

The app does not collect, transmit, or sell personal data. Workout history, circuit settings, and language preference are stored locally on your device only.

Third-party services

If you use the exercise guide search link, your browser may open an external search engine. That service is governed by its own privacy policy.

Data deletion

You can delete individual past circuits inside the app. Clearing app storage or uninstalling the app removes all local data.

Children

The app is not directed at children under 13.
